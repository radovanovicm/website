The Psychology Undergraduate Research Club

The Psychology Undergraduate Research Club (PURC) is a community for undergrads to learn more about psychological research, present to their peers, and develop professional skills outside of the laboratory. We meet approximately once a week over Zoom and our meetings include research presentations by undergraduates, research presentations by graduate students, guest lectures from faculty and industry professionals, workshops on statistical skills, and information about applying to graduate school. If you're interested, you can find more information and sign up for the listerv on the PURC website.
